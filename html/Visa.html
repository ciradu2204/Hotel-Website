Eligible Criteria for Rwanda Visa

  1. A valid passport for at least 6 months from the moment of your arrival in Rwanda
  2. A confirmed booking for accommodation – for example, a booking at a hotel. If the person travelling is being sponsored by a Rwandan citizen, they will need to produce evidence of sponsorship, stating their name, address, profession, relationship, national identity card number and telephone number.
  3. Your country should be eligible for the Rwanda E-visa
  4. Sufficient funds to meet the costs of the E-visa (US$30-100).
  5. Yellow fever vaccination and travel with vaccination certificate (children from one year).
